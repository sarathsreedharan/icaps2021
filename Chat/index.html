Instructions

The senior/junior chat event will be held at the bar in the Gathertown during the socializing hours (the exact schedule is given below). If you want to talk to one of the researchers, just look up their names in the Gathertown chat and click on follow button to locate them in the bar. If you are one of the researchers who signed up for the event, please take a table in the bar at your allocated time. Please add a * next to your name in gather town during the allocated time, so people would find it easier to identify you in Gather.
