Guide for virtual conference

If it is your first time to enter our ICAPS-21 gather space, you will be required to input your name and to choose avatar and clothing you like. Next, you are required to set up your camera, audio and microphone. Then you can enter our ICAPS-21 space by clicking "Join the Gathering".

When you are in Gather, if you were blocked in by other people, please move by holding down 'g' in your keyboard.


1. Main area

When you enter our ICAPS gather space at the first time, you will start in a main area with Canton elements. It contains five buildings: the Sessions tower, the Workshops mansion, the Bar tower, the Demo opera, and the Poster buildings. Following the red arrows, by entering the white squares, you can jump into the corresponding room. In the left-bottom side of the main area, you can find our sponsors, some of which are hiring, you might get close to the Sponsors area.


2. The Session tower

The invited talk, main sessions and competition session will be done in the Session tower. When you enter the tower, you can press 'x' in you keyboard to obtain a new webpage for living stream.


3. The Workshop building

When you enter the Workshop building, you will arrive at a hall with 6 rooms, including five rooms and a doctoral consortium room. On the wall you can find the schedule about the room. You can go back to the main area via going across the exit.


4. Workshop rooms

The plenum talk of every workshop will be held via Zoom meeting and you can press 'x' to obtain the link to the Zoom meeting. When the plenum talk is finished, you can go on with the workshop poster session or communicate with other participants freely. Here there two cases, which has been decided by the workshop organziers.

In case 1, posters have been collected and embeded in poster stands on both sides of the room. Each poster stand contains a private area where people can communicate personally. You can visit the poster stands you are interested in and the authors will be in the poster stand about their paper. It looks like attending physically.

In case 2, the poster session will be done in the form of group discussion. The workshop room contains four private areas where authors can share their screen to the participants in the same areas.

Also, in different rooms, you can find different sceneries in Guangzhou when looking at outside the window.


5. The Bar tower

The social events will happen in the Bar tower. During the confernece time, you can also take a break here.


6. The Poster buildings

The main conference poster session will be held in the Poster buildings. The Poster buildings have two entrances which are located in the front and rear respectively. Posters are arranged according to tracks. The signs on the floor can help you to find the posters you are interested in. Each poster contains a private area to allow you to communicate with the authors personally. Similarly, you can obtain a webpage containing a poster pdf via pressing 'x' in you keyboard.


7. The Demo opera

The system demonstration session will be held in the Demo opera. Here you can find 19 tables and in each table you can find a demo video via pressing 'x'.
